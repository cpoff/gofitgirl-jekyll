### **Public yoga classes:**
I am a certified and experienced registered yoga teacher in Tucson, Ariz. I teach Hatha/alignment-based public (and private) yoga classes (and indoor cycling classes) in Tucson unless otherwise noted below. I teach to all levels, whether you are a beginner, recovering from an injury, needing a slower pace with strong alignment cues, gentle adjustments and some humor I’m the yoga teacher for you. All levels are supported and welcome. All students are welcome in my classes. All students are loved. All students are safe.
